Is it wasteful for women to be housewives?

2020-09-14 1 min read

Today, I saw a hot search on whether it is wasteful for women to be a housewife. Many people in the comment section think it is a waste, because what housewives want to do is not in line with the knowledge they receive, then they are wasting the value they should give play to.

In my opinion, I strongly oppose this argument. The meaning of education is to develop one's way of thinking rather than skills. Any choice of women and the consequences of their choice are determined by their thoughts, and women should not be labelled as "wasteful" because they choose anything.
