A trip to Xian/2

2020-08-20 2 min read

In an ancient town in Xian, I met a special kind of clothing called Hanfu.

The day I met Han Fu is really my most beautiful memory forever.Not only did I learn a lot of hanfu skills from the old craftsmen in the Hanfu workshop, but I also learned about the prosperity of Chinese history and culture through hanfu.

That day I made a simple Hanfu under the guidance of the old craftsman.The moment I put it on, I really felt that I had become a inheritor of Chinese history and culture.I understand that each of us is very small, in the long river of history, it is helpless to be a walk-on, but it does not matter if we are a walk-on character.As long as the time to run with a focused mind, not because of the little words and lines and degenerate, it is enough.Perhaps day after day, will let us endure into the protagonist of the day.
