温故而知新

My experience in villages

2020-10-30 2 min read

看着一盆盆野草，我有时会想起我们这些从乡村移居到城市生活的人，尽管我们适应了盆里的生活，其实并未改变来自乡野的姿色，而所有的都市人，他们或他们的祖先，不都是来自乡野吗？只是有的人成了名花，忘记了自己的所在罢了。自然的本身是平等无二的，在乡野的山谷我们看见了自然的宏伟，在小小的花盆里，不也充满了生命的神奇吗？

Looking at the potted weeds, I sometimes think of those of us who have moved from the country to the city. Although we have adapted to the life in the pot, we have not changed the beauty of the countryside, and all the urbanites, or their ancestors, are from the countryside.It's just that some people become famous and forget where they are.Nature itself is equal, in the valley of the countryside we saw the grandeur of nature, in the small flowerpot, is not also filled with the magic of life?
