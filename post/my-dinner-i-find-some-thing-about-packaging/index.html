MY DINNER/ I FIND SOME THING ABOUT PACKAGING

2020-05-05 1 min read

I ate pasta tonight, and I found that the noodles and the ingredients' packaging is different. The bags for oil and salt are made of plastic, and the bag of meat sauce is made of aluminum. When they mix and become waste, they lost the value of recycle. I have learned that it is difficult for company to recycle different types of meterials to recycle, so there are too many foods' packaging are wasted.
